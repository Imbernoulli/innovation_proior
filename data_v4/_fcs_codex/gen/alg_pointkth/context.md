We are given up to q = 2*10^5 online operations on a multiset of integer points. Operation `1 x` inserts one occurrence of point `x`; operation `2 x` removes one occurrence of `x`, guaranteed to be present; operation `3 x k` asks for the k-th smallest value among all distances `|p - x|` over the current multiset, counting multiplicity, with at least k points guaranteed. Coordinates satisfy `|x| <= 10^9`. For every type-3 operation, we must output the requested distance. The solution is judged by exact output correctness under the time and memory limits implied by the input scale.
