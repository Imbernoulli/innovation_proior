We are given n two-stage jobs in a flow shop with two machines. Every job must run on machine A first for a_i time, then on machine B for b_i time. Each machine handles at most one job at a time, and B cannot start a job before that job finishes on A. We may choose one common job order for both machines, and must output the minimum possible completion time of the final job on B. Constraints are 1 <= n <= 2*10^5 and 1 <= a_i,b_i <= 10^9, so the answer may exceed 32-bit integer range. A correct solution must compute the exact optimum within these limits.
