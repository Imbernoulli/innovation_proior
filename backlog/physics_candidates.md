# Theoretical-physics candidate list (vetted, deduplicated)

**Scope:** ~100 THEORETICAL-PHYSICS results proposed for new discovery-reasoning traces. Every candidate's landing artifact is a derivation / formula / mechanism / theorem that is self-contained and checkable on paper — **no experiment-first results** (no measured numbers, no detection papers). All entries were grounded online (year, authors, venue, arXiv id where it exists) and deduplicated by TOPIC against `EXISTING_INVENTORY.md` (so e.g. Dirac, Noether, Yang-Mills, Higgs, BCS, Casimir, Bell, Aharonov-Bohm, Berry, IQHE/FQHE, Kosterlitz-Thouless, Landau theory, RG, special relativity, equivalence principle, Feynman path integral, no-cloning, Shor's code/algorithm, Grover, QAOA, teleportation, Holevo, Atiyah-Singer, mirror symmetry are intentionally absent).

**Per-subarea counts (total = 104):**
- Quantum field theory / high-energy theory — **22** (12 classic, 10 recent)
- Condensed-matter theory — **23** (15 classic, 8 recent)
- Gravitation & cosmology theory — **19** (13 classic, 6 recent)
- Quantum-information theory — **14** (8 classic, 6 recent)
- Statistical mechanics — **14** (9 classic, 5 recent)
- Atomic / optical & nonlinear / integrable systems — **6** (4 classic, 2 recent)
- Mathematical physics & rigorous results — **6** (4 classic, 2 recent)

Overall split = 66 classic / 38 recent (recent = last ~15 yr, 2010–2025). The recent half is concentrated in QFT (holography, bootstrap, amplitudes, generalized symmetries, swampland), condensed matter (SPT/topology classification, non-Hermitian, MBL, SYK, fracton, measurement-induced, Floquet), gravity (firewall, ER=EPR, islands/Page curve, soft theorems), and quantum information (QSVT, classical shadows, NLTS, barren plateaus). **Foundations-of-QM** (GHZ, Kochen-Specker, Gleason, PBR, Frauchiger-Renner) and a few extras are folded into the relevant subareas. **Gaps / thin spots:** atomic-optical-nonlinear and pure mathematical-physics are deliberately smaller (6 each) because much of that material is either already covered or borderline-experimental; there is room to grow both later. No candidate was rejected for being experimental in the final list, but several experiment-first papers were screened out during research (e.g. König et al. HgTe transport, LIGO detections, CMB measurements, EIT observation, loophole-free Bell tests, KPZ-class liquid-crystal experiments) — for each we kept the corresponding *theory* paper instead.
