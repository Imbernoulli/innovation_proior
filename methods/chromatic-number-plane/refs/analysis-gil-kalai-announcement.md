# Gil Kalai — "Aubrey de Grey: The chromatic number of the plane is at least 5"
Source: https://gilkalai.wordpress.com/2018/04/10/aubrey-de-grey-the-chromatic-number-of-the-plane-is-at-least-5/ (retrieved this run)

- Announcement: de Grey proved the chromatic number of the plane is at least 5 — resolving
  the lower bound that had stood between 4 and 7 since 1950.
- Core construction: a unit-distance graph that is 5-chromatic; vertex count initially
  reported as 1567, refined to 1581 after de Grey found and corrected a bug in his code.
- G = two copies of a structure S_a, with only two vertices removable without introducing
  problematic 4-colorings.
- Built on the Moser spindle (4-chromatic, 7 vertices) but required computer assistance for
  verification; independent confirmations using different algorithms including SAT solvers.
- Sparked Polymath16, seeking simpler / smaller constructions and less computer reliance.
