Introduction

This lecture covers some basic embedding results for embedding metric spaces into ℓ∞ . These ideas
are used in other embedding results, e.g., Bourgain’s theorem which provides embeddings into ℓ2 .

cannot increase the ℓ∞ norm, so the second claim also holds.
However, we cannot use less than 𝑛 − 1 coordinates without allowing some distortion.
