\subsection*{Important constants}
\begin{center}
 \begin{tabular}{|l |l |} 

 \hline\hline
 $\eta \le 10^{-12}$  & Initially, worry only about $\eta$ near mincuts of $z$\\
 & which are $2\eta$ near mincuts of $x$\\
$\eps_{1/2} = 0.0001$ & how close to 0.5 for $e$ to be half edge \\
 $\eps_\eta = 14 \eta \le \eps_{1/2}^2$ & After reduction to hierarchy using OPT edges, care\\
 & only about $\eps_\eta$ near mincuts of $x$\\
  $\eps_{1/1}= \eps_{1/2}/12  = 0.00000833 $ & $1-\eps_{1,1}$ is lower bound for A,B parts of ABC degree partitioning\\
 $p = 0.005 \eps_{1/2}^2$   & probability of 2-1-1 and 2-2-2 good events\\
 $\eps_M = 0.00025$ & perturbation of marginals with bottom edge reduction (\cref{lem:maxflow}) \\
 $\tau = 0.571 \beta$ & top edge reduction \\
 $\beta = \eta/4.1$ & bottom edge reduction\\
 $\eps_P = 3.9 \cdot 10 ^{-17}$ & final gain on each edge in O-join is $\eps_P \eta$\\
 $\eps_F = 1/10$ & constant related to $x (\delta^\uparrow(u))$ \\
 $\eps_B = 21 \eps_{1/2}$  & matching constant to handle bad edges\\
 other assumptions & $\eps_\eta \le  \eps_{1/1}/100$, $\eps_\eta \le \eps_{1/2}^2$, $\eps_\eta \le 10 ^{-10}$, $\eps_M \le 0.0005$\\
