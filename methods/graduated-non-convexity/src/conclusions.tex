\section{Conclusions}
\label{sec:conclusions}

We proposed a general-purpose approach for robust estimation that leverages modern non-minimal solvers. 
The approach allows extending the applicability of Black-Rangarajan duality and Graduated Non-convexity (\GNC) 
to several spatial perception problems, ranging from mesh registration and shape alignment to pose graph optimization.
We believe the proposed approach can be a valid replacement for \ransac. 
While \ransac requires a minimal solver, our \GNC approach requires a \emph{non-minimal} solver. 
 Our approach is deterministic, resilient to a large number of outliers, 
 and significantly faster than specialized solvers. 
 As a further contribution, we presented a non-minimal solver for shape alignment.
