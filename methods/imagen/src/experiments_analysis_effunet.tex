\subsubsection{Comparison of U-Net vs Efficient U-Net}
\label{sec:unet_vs_efficientunet}
We compare the performance of U-Net with our new Efficient U-Net on the task of $64\times64 \rightarrow 256\times256$ super-resolution task. Fig. \ref{fig:unet_vs_Efficient} compares the training convergence of the two architectures. We observe that Efficient U-Net converges significantly faster than U-Net, and obtains better performance overall. Our Efficient U-Net is also $\times2-3$ faster at sampling.
