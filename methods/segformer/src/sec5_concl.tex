\section{Conclusion}   \label{sec:concl}
In this paper, we present SegFormer, a simple, clean yet powerful semantic segmentation method which contains a positional-encoding-free, hierarchical Transformer encoder and a lightweight All-MLP decoder. 
It avoids common complex designs in previous methods, leading to both high efficiency and performance. 
SegFormer not only achieves new state of the art results on common datasets, but also shows strong zero-shot robustness. We hope our method can serve as a solid baseline for semantic segmentation and motivate further research. One limitation is that although our smallest 3.7M parameters model is smaller than the known CNN's model, it is unclear whether it can work well in a chip of edge device with only 100k memory. We leave it for future work.
