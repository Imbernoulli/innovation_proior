\section*{Broader Impact} \label{sec:impact}
Efficiency, accuracy, and robustness are essential aspects of AI models, especially for safety-essential applications. Our work significantly pushes the boundary of semantic segmentation models in those three aspects. We envision that the work will benefit a wide range of applications involving perception and recognition, such as autonomous driving and robot navigation. The proposed method improves the ``in-the-wild'' robustness of these applications, ultimately leading to better safety. In addition, since we proposed a more compact set of models, we require fewer training resources, thus reducing the carbon footprint. We do not foresee obvious undesirable ethical/social impacts at this moment.
