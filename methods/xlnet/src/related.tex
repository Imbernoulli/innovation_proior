Previous models are orderless, while XLNet is essentially order-aware with positional encodings. This is important for language understanding because an orderless model is degenerated to bag-of-words, lacking basic expressivity. The above difference results from the fundamental difference in motivation---previous models aim to improve density estimation by baking an ``orderless'' inductive bias into the model while XLNet is motivated by enabling AR language models to learn bidirectional contexts.
