\section{Conclusions}
We presented an approach for synthesizing novel renditions of a subject using a few images of the subject and the guidance of a text prompt. Our key idea is to embed a given subject instance in the output domain of a text-to-image diffusion model by binding the subject to a unique identifier.
Remarkably - this fine-tuning process can work given only 3-5 subject images, making the technique particularly accessible. 
We demonstrated a variety of applications with animals and objects in generated photorealistic scenes, in most cases indistinguishable from real images.
