\OURS is simple to implement and experiment with.
For this reason, we provide our \OURS implementation in PyTorch in this appendix.
%It is not a library, but simply a \texttt{main\_swav.py} importing model and criterion definitions with standard training loops.
From \texttt{main\_swav.py} are imported the dataset definition (\texttt{src/multicropdataset.py}), the model architecture (\texttt{src/resnet50.py}) and some miscellaneous training utilities (\texttt{src/utils.py}).
\\

For example, to train \OURS on a single node with 8 gpus for 400 epochs, run:
\\
\texttt{python -m torch.distributed.launch -{}-nproc\_per\_node=8 main\_swav.py \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-data\_path /path/to/imagenet \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-epochs 400 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-base\_lr 0.6 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-final\_lr 0.0006 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-warmup\_epochs 0 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-batch\_size 32 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-size\_crops 224 96 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-nmb\_crops 2 6 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-min\_scale\_crops 0.14 0.05 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-max\_scale\_crops 1. 0.14 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-use\_fp16 true \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-freeze\_prototypes\_niters 5005 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-queue\_length 3840 \textbackslash \\}
\texttt{-{}-epoch\_queue\_starts 15 \textbackslash \\}

Our best ResNet-50 \OURS model is available to download at \url{https://dl.fbaipublicfiles.com/deepcluster/swav_800ep_pretrain.pth.tar}.
